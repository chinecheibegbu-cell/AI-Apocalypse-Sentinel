🛡️ AI Apocalypse Sentinel
The AI that watches AI:



AI Apocalypse Sentinel is an intelligent AI-powered defense platform designed to protect individuals, organizations, and communities from the growing risks of artificial intelligence. Instead of simply using AI, the platform evaluates AI systems to improve transparency, safety, and trust.



🌍 Overview:



As AI becomes more powerful, so do AI-generated threats such as deepfakes, hallucinations, scams, phishing attacks, misinformation, and biased outputs. AI Apocalypse Sentinel helps users identify, understand, and defend against these risks through explainable and trustworthy AI analysis.



✨ Key Features



🤖 AI Hallucination Detection

🎭 Deepfake Detection (Image, Audio & Video)

📰 Fake News & Misinformation Verification

⚠️ AI Scam & Phishing Detection

📊 AI Trust Score

⚖️ Bias & Harmful Content Detection

💬 Explainable AI (Simple explanations for AI decisions)

🔍 AI Response Comparison

📈 AI Risk Forecasting

🎯 Why This Project?
AI Apocalypse Sentinel addresses one of the world's fastest-growing challenges: ensuring AI remains safe, transparent, and trustworthy. By helping users detect AI-generated threats before they cause harm, the platform promotes responsible AI adoption and digital safety.
